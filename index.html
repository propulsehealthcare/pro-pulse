Our physiotherapy program focuses on restoring mobility, strength, and function through evidence-based techniques. We help individuals recover from injuries, surgeries, chronic pain, and movement limitations using personalised treatment plans tailored to their goals.

Our expert physiotherapists use manual therapy, exercise therapy, electrotherapy, and posture correction to ensure faster, safer recovery and improved quality of life.

Whether it's post-operative recovery or chronic orthopedic pain, our comprehensive rehab accelerates healing and ensures long-term strength and stability.

Orthopedic Rehab is designed for patients recovering from fractures, joint replacements, ligament injuries, and musculoskeletal disorders. Our specialists use targeted exercises, mobility training, and pain-relief therapies to restore full joint function.

Our goal is not just temporary relief—but long-term recovery, improved posture, and a pain-free lifestyle.

We provide advanced pain-management solutions for back pain, neck pain, arthritis, sports injuries, and chronic musculoskeletal discomfort. Our treatment includes laser therapy, manual therapy, dry needling, and personalised exercise programs.

Ideal for elderly patients, bedridden individuals, and those recovering at home—our team ensures comfort, convenience, and professional medical support right at your doorstep.

Our specialised Home Care brings clinical-quality treatment to the comfort of your home. From physiotherapy and nursing care to vitals monitoring and post-operative support, we ensure safe and personalised care for every individual.

With a structured therapy approach and advanced neuro-facilitation techniques, we help patients regain independence and confidence in daily life.

Our Neuro Rehab services support individuals recovering from conditions such as stroke, spinal cord injuries, Parkinson’s disease, and neuropathies. We focus on improving balance, coordination, muscle strength, and daily functional abilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Pro Pulse Health Care & Rehabilitation Centre offer?
We provide a complete range of rehabilitation and physiotherapy services including Home Physiotherapy, Orthopedic Rehab, Neuro Rehab, Cardio-Pulmonary Rehab, Sports Physiotherapy, Paediatric & Geriatric Rehab, Manual Therapy, MFR, Cryotherapy, Laser Therapy, Pain Management, Nursing Care, and Home Care Services.
Do I need a doctor’s prescription to start physiotherapy or rehab?
No, you can directly book an appointment with our physiotherapists. However, if your condition requires specialist evaluation, our team will guide you to the right doctor or diagnostic support.
Do you provide home physiotherapy or home care services?
Yes, we offer Home Physiotherapy, Nursing Care, and Home Care Services at your doorstep. Our qualified professionals visit your home with all required equipment for safe and effective treatment.
How long does each treatment session last?
A typical physiotherapy or rehab session lasts 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the treatment type, condition severity, and individual recovery plan.
How soon can I expect results from physiotherapy or rehab?
Recovery time depends on the condition. Most patients notice improvement within 3–7 sessions, while chronic or post-operative cases may require longer treatment plans. Our therapists create a personalised rehab program for each individual.
